*Featured modules like Observation Deck help you quickly see and understand traffic and performance on your network.*

## About Featured

The Featured section in the left sidebar of the portal’s main menu offers quick access to a collection of Kentik modules that we've determined are the most commonly used or provide the most general-interest overview of the current conditions on your network.

## Featured Modules

The Featured section (leftmost column) of the portal main menu includes links to the following modules:

![](https://cdn.us.document360.io/082e25b5-afce-42d4-8f47-70bd3f1d02b7/Images/Documentation/featured menu(1).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-05-12T09%3A19%3A07Z&se=2026-05-12T09%3A31%3A07Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=s0Cc%2F5LyD0lXs1%2BPNU%2Bi4xfAy2PH45taS7AdmpL3ACw%3D)

* **Alerting**: The Alerting page shows current and historical alerts generated by Kentik’s alerting system, including the time, severity, and status of the alerts, as well as the dimensions and metrics values involved in the conditions that triggered each alert (see **Alerting**).
* **AI Advisor**: **AI Advisor** is an advanced AI agent combining data with natural language interaction to guide, troubleshoot, and provide suggestions to remediate network operation issues.
* **Kentik Map**: A visualization of your network infrastructure, both on-prem and cloud, that enables fast understanding of how components are interconnected and how that affects traffic patterns, network health, and performance, including application delivery and customer experience (see **Kentik Map**).
* **Library**: The **Library** enables both viewing and configuration of the main types of in-portal views with which you can quickly see specific aspects of interest in your network traffic. The following types of views may either be Kentik presets (created by us to address common needs) or views that are custom-created within your organization:
* **My Kentik Portal**: A page used to manage the tenants and MKP packages involved in providing your users with a limited, customer-branded version of the Kentik portal (see **My Kentik Portal**).
* **Observation Deck**: A high-level, user-configurable view of your organization's network, presented as a collection of widgets that each focus on a specific aspect of your traffic volume, performance, costs, connectivity, or utilization (see **Observation Deck**).
* **Top Talkers**: Hovering on **Top Talkers** at the bottom of the main menu’s left sidebar pops up the **Top Talkers** submenu, which provides direct access to the portal's **Core Aggregate Pages**.
* **Settings**: A landing page containing links to individual pages that each contain administrative controls for a given aspect of the Kentik system, allowing you to tailor your Kentik setup to the specific needs of your organization (see **Main Settings**). Hovering on **Settings** at the bottom of the main menu’s left sidebar pops up the Settings submenu, which allows you to go directly to an individual Settings page.
