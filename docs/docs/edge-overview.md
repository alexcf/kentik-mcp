## About Edge

The **Edge** section of the Kentik portal provides Network Operations and Engineering teams with the essential visibility and tools to manage the critical boundary between their network and the external world.

![Diagram illustrating BGP paths and peering relationships for Brazilian sites.](https://cdn.us.document360.io/082e25b5-afce-42d4-8f47-70bd3f1d02b7/Images/Documentation/DP-main-outbound-peers-brazil(1).png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2026-05-12T09%3A20%3A02Z&se=2026-05-12T09%3A31%3A02Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=3iN7YWU5z6ZQOSXAXf7qmalTySBZZRkAQ3dehvX4zfA%3D)

*Discover Peers visualizes* *your inbound/outbound network traffic and lists Potential Peers (not shown).*

This section focuses entirely on traffic exchanged with **external** networks, such as transit providers, peering partners, and Content Delivery Networks (CDNs). By analyzing both network usage (volume, direction, destination) and associated connectivity expenses, Kentik’s **Edge Modules** empower users to:

* **Optimize Costs**: Identify and reduce spend on transit by verifying invoices, establishing cost baselines, and finding free peering opportunities.
* **Improve Performance**: Reduce latency and hop count for end-users by identifying optimal peering partners and effectively routing traffic.
* **Plan Capacity**: Gain deep, data-driven insights into cost and traffic trends to ensure efficient capacity planning at key interconnection points.

> **Tip**: For information about Kentik’s Core modules, see **Core Overview**.

## Edge Modules

The below table lists the **Edge** modules along with benefits and related use cases. Follow the links for details on how to use each module.

| **Edge Module** | Benefits | Use Cases |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Traffic Costs** | Instant visibility into traffic-driven connectivity spend and real-time margin risk identification. | * Understand costs of delivering traffic from specific OTT and CDN providers. * Analyze customer margin and profitability. * Calculate costs to serve specific geographic markets or broadband ISPs. * Justify and prepare for pricing negotiations and rate increases. |
| **Connectivity Costs** | Understand key drivers of overall network spend and gain automatic cost forecasts to prevent billing surprises. | * Validate provider invoices and discover billing errors. * Calculate the effective per-Mbps costs (including fixed fees). * Establish baselines and benchmarks for interconnection costs. |
| **Traffic Engineering** | Automate data collection for engineering, allowing you to determine optimal routing and export actionable recommendations. | * Avert capacity crises and minimize use of costly backup links. * Precisely measure traffic impact on interface utilization. * Balance router loads and allocate outgoing traffic to the most performant peers. |
| **Discover Peers** | Fast discovery of peering opportunities to achieve cost optimization and performance improvement (reduced latency). | * Identify which networks to peer with and at which interconnection locations. * Predict cost and performance impacts before changes are implemented. * Monitor utilization trends to inform future capacity decisions. |
